About Our Shop

NPC is a clothing brand inspired by the popular internet meme "Non-Player Character". The brand represents the idea of being an individual who doesn't conform to societal norms and expectations. NPC offers a range of clothing items that showcase the brand's unique and edgy aesthetic, catering to those who want to express their individuality and make a statement with their fashion choices. From graphic tees to streetwear-inspired pieces, NPC has something for everyone who wants to stand out from the crowd.
